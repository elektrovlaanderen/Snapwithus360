Welcome to Snap360

I’m Tom De Backer, founder of Snapwithus360.com. Over the years, I built my experience by working for and alongside others, both in Belgium and the United States — where I am also active from Los Angeles.

With my own company, I now use this expertise to showcase real estate, businesses, and events in a distinctive way. From 3D tours and floor plans to drone imagery and professional photo & video productions, Snap360 is all about creating a strong visual experience.

Our goal? To tell your story visually and leave a lasting impression.
